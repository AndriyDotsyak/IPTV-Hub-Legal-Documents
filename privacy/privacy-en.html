Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in our IPTV Hub app.

Our application is a functional tool for playing IPTV content. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, location data, or contact information.

2. User-Provided Content

The app allows you to add M3U playlists or URLs. This data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your playlists, links, or viewing history.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play Services (for basic app functionality) and Firebase Analytics (to track crashes and app stability). These services may collect device identifiers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data, we are compliant with COPPA.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically.
